About Us

The rivalry between East Coast and West Coast rappers is one of the most famous in hip-hop history. The East Coast, home to legends like The Notorious B.I.G. and Jay-Z, shaped the gritty sound of rap in the '90s. On the other hand, the West Coast brought the laid-back, gangsta rap vibe through iconic artists like Tupac and Dr. Dre. This rivalry fueled some of the most significant moments in rap history.
